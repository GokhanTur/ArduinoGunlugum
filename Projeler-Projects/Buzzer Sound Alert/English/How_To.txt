BUZZER SOUND ALERT - How To

This project generates a beep sound using a piezo buzzer connected to an Arduino. The buzzer will beep repeatedly at 1-second intervals.

💡 Required Materials:
- Arduino Uno or compatible
- 1 × Piezo buzzer (active or passive)
- Jumper wires
- Breadboard (optional)
- USB cable

🔌 Circuit Connections:
- Connect the positive leg of the buzzer to digital pin 8.
- Connect the negative leg of the buzzer to GND.

🖥️ Steps:
1. Open the Arduino IDE.
2. Connect the Arduino board to your PC.
3. Paste the code into the IDE.
4. Upload the code to your Arduino.
5. The buzzer will produce periodic beep sounds.
